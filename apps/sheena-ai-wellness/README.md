# Sheena — AI Wellness & NEET PG Companion

A beautiful, AI-powered wellness companion and NEET PG study planner built for Sheena.

## Features

### Wellness
- 💬 **AI Emotional Counselor** — Talk to Sheena about anything. Powered by Gemini/Claude/OpenAI.
- 🔊 **AI Voice Responses** — Sheena speaks her replies using your device's TTS.
- 🧠 **Mood Tracker** — Log moods with notes, streak tracking, and weekly charts.
- 🌬️ **Breathing & Grounding** — 4-7-8, box breathing, and 5-4-3-2-1 grounding exercises.
- 💪 **Workout Planner** — Build workouts, AI-generated plans, and exercise logging.
- 📓 **Private Journal** — Completely offline, safe space for your thoughts.

### NEET PG Study Planner
- 📚 **Full Core BTR Schedule** — All topics, subtopics, and test dates from May–August 2026.
- ✅ **Progress Tracking** — Check off subtopics as you complete them.
- ⏰ **Study Timer** — Pomodoro and deep focus timers.
- 📄 **AI Document Analyzer** — Upload photos of notes/textbooks and get AI summaries + Q&As.
- 🤖 **AI Study Assistant** — Ask for revision checklists, topic explanations, or motivation.

### Health & Nutrition
- 💧 **Water Tracker** — Tap glasses to log hydration, daily goals, streaks, and reminders.
- 🍎 **Food Logger** — Log meals by text or photo. AI estimates calories & macronutrients.
- 📊 **AI Health Analytics** — Correlations between food, mood, sleep, water, and study focus.

### Smart Features
- 🔔 **Notifications** — Water reminders, study break alerts, daily mood check-ins.
- 🔈 **Alexa Export** — Export study schedule as Alexa routines or calendar (.ics) files.
- 🔑 **Configurable AI Key** — Use your own Gemini (free), Claude, or OpenAI API key.
- 📥 **Data Export** — Backup all your data as JSON anytime.

## Getting Started (Desktop)

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari).
2. Tap ⚙️ Settings to add your free Gemini API key from [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey).
3. Start using all AI-powered features!

## Android App Build

```bash
cd apps/sheena-ai-wellness/android
# Requires Android Studio and SDK 34
./gradlew assembleDebug
```

The APK will be at `app/build/outputs/apk/debug/app-debug.apk`.

## Data Privacy

- All personal data (journal, mood, health logs) stays on your device.
- Only AI chat and image analysis are sent to your chosen AI provider.
- Your API key is stored locally and never shared.
