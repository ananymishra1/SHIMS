# Privacy Policy — SHIMS Personal AI

**Effective Date:** May 29, 2026

**JK Lifecare Technologies** ("we", "us", "our") operates the SHIMS Personal AI mobile application (the "App").

## Our Core Promise

**SHIMS Personal AI is designed to be private by default.**

- Your conversations happen on your device.
- We do not collect, store, or transmit your chat history.
- We do not use your data for advertising or AI training.
- No account or email is required to use the app.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** collect:
- Chat messages or conversations
- Voice recordings
- Personal memories, notes, or reminders
- Documents uploaded for RAG
- Device identifiers for tracking
- Location data
- Analytics or crash data (unless you explicitly opt in)

## Information Stored Locally

All data is stored **only on your device**:

| Data Type | Storage | Sync |
|-----------|---------|------|
| Chat history | Device storage | Optional (premium only) |
| Memories | SQLite (device) | Optional (premium only) |
| Notes | SQLite (device) | Optional (premium only) |
| Reminders | SQLite + Android alarms | No |
| Downloaded AI models | External storage | No |
| Settings | SharedPreferences | No |

## Optional Cloud Features (Premium)

If you subscribe to SHIMS Premium and enable cloud sync:
- Your data is encrypted end-to-end before transmission.
- We cannot read your encrypted data.
- You can delete all cloud data at any time from Settings.

## Model Downloads

AI models are downloaded from public repositories (HuggingFace). These downloads:
- Are initiated by you explicitly
- Are stored on your device only
- Are not transmitted to us

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the App and on our website.

For privacy questions: privacy@jklifecare.com
