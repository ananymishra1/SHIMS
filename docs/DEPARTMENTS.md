# Department Workflow Blueprint

## R&D

- Product-wise experiment pipeline.
- Planned, running, completed, failed statuses.
- Objective, hypothesis, parameters, observations, result summary.
- AI suggestions for controls, variables, risk, and next trial.

## QC

- Editable COA templates.
- Template fields include parameter, unit, specification, method, and required flag.
- COA records use templates and can export DOCX certificates.

## Warehouse

- Material master.
- Entry, exit, and adjustment movements.
- Current stock and reorder levels.
- Vendor linkage and stock warnings.

## Production

- Batch number, product, planned quantity, actual quantity.
- Stage, status, QC status, blockers.

## Procurement

- Material requests.
- Quantity, unit, priority, reason, status.
- Vendor suggestions and procurement dashboard.

## Executive

- Harmonic overview of experiments, QC, warehouse, production, and procurement.
